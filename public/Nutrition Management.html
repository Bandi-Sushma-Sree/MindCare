Recognizing Malnutrition: Common Symptoms

Physical Symptoms

Fatigue, weakness, weight loss, and poor wound healing.

Cognitive Symptoms

Difficulty concentrating, memory loss, confusion, and mood changes.

Health-Related Symptoms

Weakened immune system, hair loss, skin problems, and digestive issues.
