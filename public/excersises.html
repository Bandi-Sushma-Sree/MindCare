Mental Health Exercises

Deep Breathing

A simple technique to reduce stress and increase calmness.

  • Find a comfortable position
  • Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 seconds
  • Hold your breath for 4 seconds
  • Exhale slowly through your mouth for 4 seconds
  • Repeat for 5-10 minutes

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Reduce tension and achieve relaxation by tensing and relaxing muscle groups.

  • Start with your toes and work your way up to your head
  • Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds
  • Relax the muscle group and notice the difference
  • Move to the next muscle group
  • Repeat the process for your entire body

Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on the present moment to reduce anxiety and increase awareness.

  • Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit
  • Close your eyes and focus on your breath
  • Notice the sensation of breathing without trying to change it
  • When your mind wanders, gently bring your attention back to your breath
  • Continue for 10-15 minutes
